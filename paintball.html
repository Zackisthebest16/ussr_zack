This is easy and fun sport if you love being shot at. If you want to start playing paint then there are some things you should know about before you start playing and you will learn some fun facts. The correct term for a paintball gun is “paintball marker”. The first paintball guns were never intended to be used on people. In fact, they were used to mark trees and cattle–hence the term, “paintball marker”. Cattlemen use paintball markers to mark cattle within a herd.The winner of the first game of paintball won the game without firing a shot.In 2006, paintball was the third most popular extreme sport, with more than 10 million players.Paintball is played in more than 100 countries, across six continents.The largest manufacturer of paintball guns in the United States is Tippmann Sports in Ft. Wayne, IN.Paintballs travel at speeds up to 200 miles per hour.The fill inside a paintball is not actually made of paint.Paintballs are made of non-toxic, bio-degradable materials. You could eat one, but it tastes terrible.Anthony Kelly of Australia holds the only paintball world record, as recognized by the Guinness Book Of World Records. He was able to catch 11 unbroken paintballs, while blindfolded, in two minutes.Paintball technology is also used by military forces, law enforcement, para-military and security organizations to supplement military training, as well as playing a role in riot response, and non-lethal suppression of dangerous suspects.
